## Copy rules for whoever implements this

1. **The "weight / carry / pressure" metaphor appears only TWICE** on the page — hero subhead and final CTA. Strip it everywhere else.
2. **Every CTA on the page should be one of three verbs:** *See* (assessment), *Try* (Virtual Mind), *Read* (about/framework). No "Lead Free Now" buttons — too abstract for a click.
3. **No paragraph longer than 3 lines on desktop.** Break with line returns.
4. **Every stat needs a year, source, or qualifier.** "7× ROI" alone is unbelievable. "7× ROI — average across 2024 Fortune-level clients" is.
5. **Mobile:** the Five Tensions and 4 Profile cards should each be a horizontal swipe carousel, not a stacked list — they're scannable assets, not paragraphs.
