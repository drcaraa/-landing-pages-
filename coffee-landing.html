The RPR Method, Before Breakfast

Receive. Perceive. Respond.

The RPR Method is the operational core of Connected Leadership. GroundID Coffee is designed to be the first deliberate run of it, every morning, before the day has a chance to run you.

01 Receive
The cup in your hand is the signal to slow receive.

Not the next email. Not the group chat. Something warm, earned, and quiet. Your nervous system registers it. That registration is the opening. From here, you can receive what the day is actually offering rather than what you are bracing for.

02 Perceive
One honest question before you start.

What part of me is leading right now? The Architect trying to control? The Carrier absorbing? The Performer producing? The Sentinel on alert? Perception begins the moment you can name what is running the show. GroundID creates the 60-second window where that naming is possible.

03 Respond
Enter the day on purpose, not on autopilot.

Response is not reaction. It is the choice that follows naming. One intention. One boundary. One posture for the day. Grounded leaders do not lead harder. They lead from a place that was set earlier, on purpose, with something as small as a cup of coffee.
