About GroundID Leader

Most leadership development starts with behavior — what to say, how to delegate, how to give feedback. That work is not wrong. It is incomplete. It addresses the surface while the driver underneath stays untouched.

GroundID Leader is a 12-week Connected Leadership cohort built on 25 years of clinical and executive practice. We work at the driver level — the origin point that formed long before the title, the team, or the role — so the shift moves from language into practice.
